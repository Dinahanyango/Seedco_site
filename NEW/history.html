HISTORY OF BIG MINDS UNIVERSITY



The school was started in 19th march 2006 as a result of a group six people who came with an idea of starting a college to help the local people to get skills which could help them. The group then presented their idea to the ministry of education to be granted permmission to start the institution. After they were granted the permission a land was bought in Baguela town which is todays location of the university.The school's population turned out very well after two years that is in 2008. In january 2010 the school advanced and it was made a university. The school has now spread as it has three braches which are;
  • Rucheru campus

  • Tioro campus

  • Matandu campus
